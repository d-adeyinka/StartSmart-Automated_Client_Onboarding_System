  <h1>Client Onboarding Automation – “StartSmart”</h1>

  <section>
    <p><strong>StartSmart</strong> is a next-generation client onboarding automation designed to give your clients a smooth, consistent, and engaging onboarding experience. Built for service-based businesses looking to scale operations without sacrificing quality, this agent streamlines client intake, communication, document management, and internal task tracking by combining intelligent workflow automation with Google Workspace tools.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h2>What This Agent Does</h2>
    <p><strong>StartSmart</strong> acts as a digital onboarding assistant. When a client fills out your Google Form, the agent:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Sends a personalized welcome email.</li>
      <li>Creates a structured project folder in Google Drive with pre-defined subfolders (Project Brief, Design Assets, Deliverables, Reports).</li>
      <li>Updates Google Sheets to track onboarding status and internal tasks.</li>
      <li>Schedules a one-hour discovery call based on the client’s preferred date and time.</li>
      <li>Optionally sends follow-up emails or onboarding packages.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>This ensures every client receives consistent communication and that internal teams have clear visibility on next steps, reducing manual errors and improving operational efficiency.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
     <h2>Key Capabilities</h2>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Automated Welcome Emails:</strong> Personalized emails triggered immediately after form submission.</li>
      <li><strong>Smart Calendar Scheduling:</strong> Automatically books a one-hour discovery call in Google Calendar using the client’s preferred time.</li>
      <li><strong>Structured Document Management:</strong> Google Drive folders automatically created with all necessary subfolders for each client.</li>
      <li><strong>Internal Task Tracking:</strong> Google Sheets updated automatically to reflect client onboarding status (Pending, Successful, Declined).</li>
      <li><strong>Follow-up Automation:</strong> Optional follow-up emails or delivery of onboarding packages to maintain engagement.</li>
      <li><strong>Centralized Progress Monitoring:</strong> Provides management with real-time visibility into onboarding completion rates.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>
